These Celebs Have Aged Flawlessly

TRENDING MAY 16, 2023

Lea Thompson – Age 61

You may remember Lea Thompson from Back to the Future or perhaps a more recent role like starring in Little Women or Over The Hill. Whenever we look at Lea, her look reveals a beautiful natural glow. According to an interview with the Chicago Sun-Times in 2014, she doesn’t smoke or drink while keeping her beauty treatments simple, and it sure is working.

Fans saw Lea playing a small part in the hit series The Goldbergs during 2020 while she also starred in Dinner in America while looking great. We’re also looking forward to her upcoming comedy Mark, Mary & Some Other People, scheduled for a 2021 release.

Demi Moore – Age 60

When the green-eyed brunette Demi Moore first appeared in the movies, she captivated viewers with more than just her stunning appearance. After all, she was one of the most talented actresses of the ’80s. It’s no wonder that, by 1996, Moore was getting a record $12.5 million deal to star in an Andrew Bergman movie.

From then on, her career never lost its momentum. And neither did her looks. Captivating the attention of the likes of Bruce Willis and Ashton Kutcher, Moore still looks impressive enough to be called a bombshell. We never thought that being 57 could ever look this good!

Erika Eleniak – Age 53

It takes a special lady to rock the red swimsuit in the Baywatch TV series, especially considering people like Pamela Anderson and David Hasselhoff were also part of the cast. But to the stunning Erika Eleniak, being among the world’s hunkiest actors wasn’t intimidating at all; in fact, it felt natural.

Many things have changed in Eleniak’s life since her Baywatch days. But not the fact that she can feel proud of herself anytime she takes a look at the mirror. Now 50, she continues to work as an actress and has recently appeared in the TV series Desperate Housewives and the movie Boone: The Bounty Hunter.

Jeri Ryan – Age 55

When she first appeared dressed up as Seven of Nine in Star Trek: Voyager, the stunning actress Jeri Ryan immediately gained the hearts and souls of every sci-fi fan out there. But who could’ve imagined that the West Germany-born American actress would still look as good at the age of 52?

If staying beautiful is an art, Ryan is the embodiment of a masterpiece. Just like a Michelangelo Fresco, she’s a mesmerizing image you cannot look away from. Still acting, she has recently made it to the movie, Devil’s Revenge, and has appeared in the 2020 reboot of MacGyver.

Pauley Perrette – Age 53

The “punk-rocker” Pauley Perrette doesn’t have to dress girly to make every man sigh. Proving that you don’t need to like Barbie dolls to know how to take good care of yourself, this tomboy American actress is still looking gorgeous past the age of 50.

Working as an actress, singer, writer, and civil rights advocate, the beautiful and talented Perrette is best-known for her role as Abby Sciuto in NCIS. She left the show in 2018 to join Broke, a one-season 2020 TV series in which Perrette plays one of the leading roles.
