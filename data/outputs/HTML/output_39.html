      Everything ‘going against’ Joe Biden as poll numbers ‘continue to get worse’

      10 hours ago

      Former White House Chief of Staff Mick Mulvaney says everything is “going against” Joe Biden right now as poll numbers in the US continue to get worse for the President.

      "Certainly, it's been a tough couple of weeks – if not couple of months – for the Biden administration," Mr Mulvaney told Sky News Australia.

      "The numbers continue to get worse."

      Mr Mulvaney was asked about the factors impacting President Biden's slide in the polls.

      “It’s everything going against Biden right now,” he said.

      “Inflation is not helping him – he’s also losing the support of some of the pro-Palestinian wings of his party – the Democrat Party is very closely aligned with Palestinian movement here in the country.

      “So, there’s a lot of Democrats who aren’t happy with the president’s handling of the situation in Gaza."

      Mr Mulvaney said President Biden’s age is also having an impact.

      “You can change a policy on spending that might help on inflation, you can change a policy on Gaza that might help make part of your base happy," he said.

      “But you’re never going to change the fact that you just turned 81 last week and that is a bigger and bigger issue with every growing day.”
