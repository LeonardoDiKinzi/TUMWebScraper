Take a Look at The Most Jaw-Dropping Celebrity Private Jets & Yachts

TRENDING APRIL 27, 2023

Bill Gates – Bombardier BD-700 Global Express, Estimated $40 Million

Bill Gates is one of the most famous businessmen in America. He made his fortune as the co-founder of Microsoft and has since become a major investor and philanthropist.

With a net worth of $89 billion, you would think that Tech mogul Bill Gates would have one private jet for each day of the week. Instead, he chose a modest $40 million Bombardier BD-700 Global Express and equipped it with millions of dollars in gadgets and innovations – because, well…he happens to be a money-smart businessman. That said, we should expect the billionaire to go plane shopping pretty soon because a man of his stature cannot have enough private jets.

Enrique Iglesias – Gulfstream IV, Estimated $3.6 Million

Enrique Iglesias is one of the most popular Spanish singers and has entertained audiences worldwide with his passionate music. Although he was born in Spain, Enrique has achieved worldwide success.

Global heartthrob Iglesias has been on the music scene for quite a while – and being able to belt out beautiful tunes has its many dividends. From a bank account that is clearly bursting at the seams to a $3.6-million Gulfstream IV, Enrique is apparently living it up. To top things off, the singer is a certified pilot – which seems like an effort to see that his money does not lay idle. Talk about a man of many talents!

Jerry Seinfeld – Gulfstream V, Estimated $22 million

Considered one of the world’s funniest men, Jerry Seinfeld has carved a unique niche for himself in Hollywood. In addition to playing himself in the iconic American sitcom, Seinfeld, Jerry has dabbled in acting, producing, and directing – all of which have been instrumental in building his portfolio and stuffing his bank account. So when the comic icon wanted to splurge on a $22-million Gulfstream V, he certainly didn’t need a financial advisor to direct his affairs. Well, after more than four decades in the industry, he deserves the luxury.

Tiger Woods – Privacy Yacht, Estimated $20 Million

When most people think of professional golfers, the first name that comes to mind is most definitely Tiger Woods. He is one of the most successful and well-known athletes in history, and his accomplishments on the course are astounding.

Even though his ex-wife made away with a small chunk of his earnings during their divorce, pro-golfer Tiger Woods clearly held on to a few of his priced properties, including his yacht, which he quite aptly dubbed Privacy. The vessel provides room for 21 guests, and there’s a gym, theater, Jacuzzi, lounge bar, jet skis, and water sports equipment. It should undoubtedly provide him with all the ‘privacy’ he needs.

Matthew McConaughey – Bombardier Global 7000, Estimated $72.8 Million

Matthew McConaughey is an American actor who has starred in some of the biggest and most popular films of recent years. From his breakthrough role in Dazed and Confused to more recent hits such as The Wolf of Wall Street and Dallas Buyers Club. Leading Hollywood Texan McConaughey has been topping charts for as long as we can remember – and our favorite onscreen heartthrob certainly deserves a luxurious life on account of the many times he has delivered heartwarming performances.

So his $72.8-million Bombardier Global 7000 private jet is simply an embodiment of his dividends of labor. With an investment like that, this massive troupe is understandable.
